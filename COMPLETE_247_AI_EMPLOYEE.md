```

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

   "Bhai, yeh reply bhejun?" (Urdu)

   - "Haan, bhej do" ✅
   - "Nahi, revise karo" ❌
   - "Edit karna hai" ✏️
   ↓
8. Qwen acts accordingly
   ↓
9. Logs in audit trail
   ↓
10. Moves to Done/
```

## 🔧 **CONFIGURATION:**

### **Language Preferences:**

Create `config/language_prefs.txt`:
```txt
# Primary Language (for Qwen communication)
PRIMARY_LANGUAGE=Urdu

# Auto-Detect Email Language
AUTO_DETECT=true

# Reply in Same Language as Sender
REPLY_IN_SAME_LANGUAGE=true

# Supported Languages
SUPPORTED_LANGUAGES=Urdu,Roman_Urdu,English,Arabic,Chinese,Japanese,Spanish,German,Italian,French,Portuguese,Russian

# Fallback Language (if detection fails)
FALLBACK_LANGUAGE=English
```
